Museum Art App

An interactive platform to make exploring art engaging and accessible using camera-based capture and virtual collections.

Role: UI/UX Designer | Tools: Figma | Timeline: 2 weeks

Brief

For my project, I created the Museum Art App, an interactive platform designed to make exploring art more engaging and accessible. Using the app’s built-in camera feature, users can capture artworks to instantly access detailed information and high-quality images. They can also explore virtual museum collections and receive personalized recommendations based on their interests.

Screenshots

Problems

  • How might we focus on education and engagement?
  • How might we focus on technology and innovation?
  • How might we focus on accessibility and cultural connection?

Goals

  • Allow people to enhance their art exploration
  • Make accessible learning possible from home
  • Make the application more interactive

Solution

UI screens of the app — showcasing camera capture, details pages, and virtual collections.

Research questions

  • People showed interest in interactive digital tools that make art learning fun and memorable.
  • Art enthusiasts appreciated apps that provide visual storytelling and background context about artworks or artists.
  • A successful app should blend education and experience, allowing users to connect emotionally with art.

Insights

  • People value interactive and modern features that make art feel more engaging and less intimidating.
  • Some visitors want to continue exploring or learning about art even after leaving the museum.
  • Many visitors prefer a simple, visual app that helps them save and organize their favorite artworks.

Opportunities

  • Create a photo-based artwork log where users can capture pieces using the camera.
  • Integrate sharing options so users can post selected artworks or museum moments on social platforms.
  • Include a map feature that highlights museums and nearby exhibits.
  • Offer a section to save favorite artworks, exhibitions, and recently viewed pieces.
  • Provide personalized recommendations based on viewing history, interests, or museum locations.
